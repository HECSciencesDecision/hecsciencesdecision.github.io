    Personalized Dynamic Pricing with Machine Learning: High Dimensional Features and Heterogeneous Elasticity

    Gah-Yi Ban · Feb 24, 2023

    Date: vendredi 24 février 2023 Heure: 10h-11h Webinaire Zoom Résumé: We consider a seller who can dynamically adjust the price of a product at the individual customer level, by utilizing information about customers’ characteristics encoded as a $d$-dimensional feature vector. We assume a personalized demand model, parameters of which depend on $s$ out of the $d$ features. The seller initially does not know the relationship between the customer features and the product demand, but learns this through sales observations over a selling horizon of $T$ periods.
